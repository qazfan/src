Welcome

CSS is Ρoetry is a guild for web coding and design. Here you can chat, get code help, improve your skills, and get feedback about your work.

Join

CiP is now a public guild, so joining is easy. Please introduce yourself on the guild board as soon as you join.

Ranks

Note that ranks earned before August 8th, 2012, were part of a different system.

New Member: Default

Nifty: Thirty posts and one month of membership

Spiffy: One hundred posts, four months of membersip, and a pin

Superlative: Three hundred posts, one year of membership, and three pins

Council: Closed

Activities

You can do the following activities at any time and in any order. They don't have deadlines. When you complete an activity, post about your finished project on the guild board. NeoMail Qazfan for your pin.

1

Code a user lookup or pet lookup that uses your own images to replace your pets, account age shield, or another image. Remember that it is against the rules to cover or retype user/pet stats or actual neopet name/color, to pretend you have a species or color of pet you don't, to pretend you have items you don't own, or to pretend your account it older/younger than it is.

2

Make a code gallery. It's just like an art page, but it's to showcase your finest coding examples.

3

Do a user lookup trade with a guild member.

4

Do a coding project that takes over a hundred thousand characters of code.

4.522

Recruit a new member!

5

Whenever you make a new page, submit it by NeoMail to Qazfan to be featured here. The best page each month gets a pin!

Pins

You can get pins from activities, benchmarks, and crazy random happenstances, among other things. You can display them here and anywhere else you please.

Pins are not awarded automatically. NeoMail Qazfan to collect yours! (Topic: Pins | Message: Gimme gimme! +Optional details) There's at least one for everybody even now, in addition to the pins you have yet to earn, so don't hesitate to redeem it.

Furthermore, you can link to your petpages in your pins section using a 50x50 link or a blank pin. NeoMail Qazfan to add them.

Rules

Be civil. If you must bicker, do it over NeoMail and not on the guild board.

Be literate. Do not let years of reading and writing education go to waste!

Post thoughtfully. Posts don't have to be strictly code related, but they should be relevant and interesting to other guild members. Limit yourself to one or two posts in a row, and if your post doesn't take up more than a line, stop to consider whether it's worth posting at all and whether you can add something.
