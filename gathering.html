Kain is a pathetic shut in. He doesn't like to leave the house. He barely talks to his siblings. He has no friends. He is ace and aro. He spends all his free time gardening and reading. While caring for his plants he tends to look angry when he focuses. While reading he either has a blank expression or he's sobbing uncontrollably. Kain cries a lot. He's my little emo boi and I love him so. I made this xwee in 2005 and he is my muse. He wears a unique necklace looped around his neck, and it looks like a silver crow palm wrapped around a purple iridescent marble. A crow demon is trapped inside this pendant, and when it haunts him he manifests black wings. His eyes are pale blue violet. His hair is dyed in sky blue and pink violet stripes. He has scars down both cheeks created by fingernails dragging down his skin. When his bangs get too long he uses hair pretties. If you draw him you can give him new hair pretties. Make him sad.
