Wut is a hipster?

A hipster is a person who makes a point of avoiding mainstream things, plays an instrument and/or gets a liberal arts degree, and spends a large amout of time at coffee shops and obscure concerts.

Because hipsters love being unique, they hate being called hipsters; it implies that there are thousands of other people just like them. Since hipsters don't usually own that they're hipsters, they don't usually make hipster how to guides. I have come to terms with the term, however, so I am at liberty to create this page.

What do hipsters wear?

  • Scarves
  • Big black glasses
  • Skinny jeans
  • Second hand clothing
  • Clothing that looks like it's second hand
  • Hoodies
  • T-shirst for obscure indie bands

Habits

  • Drink coffee
  • Write poety and/or deeply meaningful novels
  • Play instruments (good choices are jazz instruments, synths, or instruments that no one has heard of before)
  • Follow indie bands
  • Attend liberal arts colleges
  • Ride one-speed bikes instead of driving
  • Say everything sarcastically

Events

  • Shows, but only shows for bands that no one has heard of
  • Poetry slams
  • Film festivals
  • Art events

Forbidden events (Unless you do it ironically)

  • Prom
  • Altador Cup
  • Twisted Roses concert
  • Anything related to Twilight/vampires/werewolves/etc.

Haunts

  • Catacombs
  • Cafes
  • Art Gallery
  • Jelly World
  • You won't have heard of it...

Lingo

  • indie: independant, not associated with a big record label or corporation
  • sell-out: indie no longer
  • vegan: won't eat any animal products (meat, eggs, milk, ice cream, etc.)
  • pescetarian: vegetarian except for seafood
  • early adopter: someone who did/wore/said/followed something before it was cool
  • show: concert
  • hepster: Before there were hipsters, there were hepsters.
  • ironic: There's Merriam-Webster irony, literary irony, notactually irony, and hipster irony. Hipster irony is what justifies doing something that a hipster wouldn't normally do. Example: I'm going to the NC Mall ironically.
